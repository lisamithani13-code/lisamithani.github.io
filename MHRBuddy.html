Research

The MHR Challenge & The Patient Journey

Australia's "My Health Record" (MHR) system was introduced to improve information sharing, but it suffers from low patient awareness, a separate account creation process, and technical documentation that is hard for patients to understand.

Target Persona

Our research focused on patients aged 45 and above with chronic diseases, who frequently navigate the complex journey between GPs and specialists.

Demographic: Arthur Thompson, 48, Sydney Inner West
Illness Type: Type 2 Diabetes, Hypertension

A Patient's End-to-End Journey (Current State)

To understand the real-world friction, we interviewed over 15 nurses, doctors, and MHR users at the hackathon to map out the current journey.

1

Accessing MHR

Patients face a complex setup requiring a separate MHR account, even with a MyGov login. There is a general lack of awareness about the platform's purpose and benefits.

2

GP Visits

Patients must repeatedly provide their medical history at each visit. A lack of transparent specialist information often limits their choices to the GP's immediate recommendation.

3

Specialist Visits

Long wait times are common, and the medical documentation provided by specialists in the MHR is often too technical for patients to understand, leading to confusion.

The Vision

The Ideal Patient Journey with MHR Buddy

üíª

Accessing MHR

  • Unified MyGov login with IHI link. No need to create a new account.
  • MHR helps patients manage appointments and understand medical symptoms.

GP Visits

  • GP accesses patient data directly via MHR, so the patient doesn't need to repeat themselves.
  • Patient can select a specialist with GP's help, based on history, availability, and location.
  • Appointments are booked and documents sent by the GP through MHR, so patients don't need to manage it themselves.

Specialist Visits

  • Patients can chat with AI to ask follow-up questions and receive reminders.
  • Appointment notifications are sent via MHR, improving visibility and care coordination.
The Solution

MHR Buddy: A Personalised Health Companion

üîë
Unified & Simplified Access

The redesigned dashboard provides a clear, patient-centric overview of the entire care journey. Key features are brought to the forefront, including: View Consultations, AI assistance, Visual Reports, and Privacy Controls.

ü§ñ
AI-Powered Assistance

The core of MHR Buddy is an AI assistant that translates complex medical documents into easy-to-understand summaries. Patients can ask questions about their consultations, medications, and symptoms in plain language.

Simplifying Consultations

MHR Buddy transforms dense, technical medical documents into clean, glanceable summaries. Patients can quickly see key takeaways and prescribed actions, empowering them to manage their care effectively.
